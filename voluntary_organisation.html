Recommendations for the selection of NGO's
  • It shall be mandatory to be registered under the Society Registration Act or charity act or Companies Act (Section-25) for concerned NGO.
  • Work in the public sector for at least three years shall remain binding for concerned NGO.
  • It is mandatory for NGO to submit last three years audit report from Comptroller and Auditor General and CA from panel of Government of India along with the application.
  • It is mandatory for the NGO to submit the bond showing their financial capability for future repairs and maintenance of the project.
  • Quality, social work and development of the NGO who has done work related to SC and Neo Buddhist historic eminent personalities would be taken into consideration while selecting an application. It is a right of the selecting committee to consider such NGOs as special matter while choosing proposal.
  • There should not be more than two officials of NGO belonging to the same family / blood relatives. Affidavit regarding this should be submitted.
  • Benefits of approved project should be taken only by maximum SC & Neo Buddhist community and it should be ensured by Assistant Commissioner & social welfare officers.
  • All the officers and members of the NGO should belong to Scheduled Castes or Neo-Buddhists category only.
  • projects from states other than Maharashtra shall not be approved under this scheme
  • Only NGO’s run by Neo Buddhist converted from scheduled castes of Maharashtra will remain eligible for the scheme. And shall not be eligible for NGO’s run by neo Buddhists converted from other castes of Maharashtra.
  • The officers and members of the concerned NGO shall not be from any other castes than scheduled cast and the maximum benefits of project should be taken by scheduled caste community only. Joint Affidavit regarding this should be submitted by president and secretary of NGO.
  • Caste Certificate and Caste Validity Certificate are binding on all the members of the NGO concerned.
Project Selection Criteria
  • The area on which the project will be constructed should be owned by concerned NGO. In this regard, the relevant documents shall be submitted by the officials of the NGO.
  • Cultural centers, vippasana centers, meditation centers, libraries, training centers, study centers, roads, water tanks, etc construction could be done by NGO under this scheme,.
  • Culturally important places could be developed under this scheme. However, monuments of eminent person belonging to Scheduled Castes and Neo-Buddhists can be constructed at the site/place related to that person only. Projects based on cultural and ethical value development could be constructed anywhere in the state. It is not necessary to prove the cultural and historical importance of such projects.
  • Details of exact usage to be done of project constructed under this scheme should be included in proposal.
  • Location of such projects under this scheme should be convenient for everyone to visit.
